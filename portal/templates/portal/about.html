    What is UniBind?

    UniBind is a comprehensive database of transcription factor binding sites predicted through uniform processing of thousands of ChIP-seq datasets by using three different peak callers (MACS, BCP and HOMER) and five different prediction models (PWM, DiMO, TFFM, DNAShape and Energy).
